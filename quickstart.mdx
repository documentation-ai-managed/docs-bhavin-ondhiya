## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- A valid email address for account verification
- `<5` minutes of your time

</Callout>

No software installation required—everything runs in your browser.

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up for Bhavin Ondhiya.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="external-link">

    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com/signup`.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Your Details" icon="user">

    Enter your full name, email address, and a secure password. Agree to the terms of service.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">

    Check your inbox (and spam folder) for a verification email from Bhavin Ondhiya. Click the link to activate your account.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

    Choose a strong password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

</Callout>

## Log In to Your Account

Access your dashboard using one of these methods.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email & Password" icon="lock">
    Enter your email and password on the login page at `https://dashboard.example.com/login`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google OAuth" icon="globe">
    Click "Continue with Google" and select your Google account. This method skips password entry.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Once logged in, you land on the main dashboard.

## Create Your First Project

Now, set up your initial documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a New Project" icon="plus">

    From the dashboard, click the "New Project" button in the top right.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Project Settings" icon="settings">

    Provide a project name like "My First Docs" and a brief description. Add the required YAML frontmatter to your first page.

````jsx
<CodeGroup tabs="MDX">
  ```yaml
---
title: Introduction
description: Welcome to my project
---
  ```
</CodeGroup>
````

    Save and preview to see your page render.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Your Project" icon="upload">

    Click "Publish" to make your documentation live. Share the generated URL with your team.

  </Step>
</Steps>

Your project is now ready for editing.
