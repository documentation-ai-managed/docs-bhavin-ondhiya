---
title: Editor Not Saving Changes
description: Steps to fix when changes do not save in the editor.
---

## Symptoms

- Edits aren't reflected after saving.
- Save button appears unresponsive.

## Possible Causes

- Session timeout
- Browser issue

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh browser">
    ````
    Save your current work elsewhere and reload the page.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-login">
    ````
    Log out and sign in again to refresh your session.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different browser">
    ````
    Use Chrome, Firefox, or Edge for best compatibility.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
